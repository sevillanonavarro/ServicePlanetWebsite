WHO WE ARE

ABOUT COMPANY

Serviceplanet is a provider of services for customers, and operates as a “white label” service. The company manages the brand which we offer as services related to connected devices, always making their brand visible. We specialize in connected electronics, solve problems with phones, computers, TVs and routers.

COMPANY HISTORY

OUR HISTORY

Founded as a spinoff of Dunnet ITC with four founding members in 2005, when they wanted to take a step into computer repair.

Dunnet’s technical service became independent and Service Planet was born in Achterklooster 3 where everything started. Open Source software is the foundation on which Service Planet has been built since its foundation.

Within a few months, an ERP application was set up in which Service Planet can carry out its repair activities. The team grew to eleven people. Service Planet made the crossing to Rotterdam South. At Korte Stadionweg 103A.

The company grew to more than thirty employees and outgrew its jacket. In June 2011, Service Planet therefore moved to its new location at Stuttgart- and Bristolstraat on the Noord-West business park.
