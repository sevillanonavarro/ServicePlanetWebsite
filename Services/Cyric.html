CYRIC

Glad you found our page about Cyric! We on behalf of Service Planet hope that you will find what you are looking for here. We try to provide you with additional knowledge about Cyric and the functionalities it offers through this page. Cyric has grown into a European product over the years. We are very proud of this! Are you stuck? Or do you not find the answer to your question? Please feel free to contact us! The Cyric team is ready for you.

WHAT IS CYRIC?

Cyric is a toolset with 6 functionalities:

  • Hardware test
  • Create image
  • Restore image
  • Malware scan
  • Data vault
  • Data wipe

The toolset supports the x86 platform. This includes most desktops and notebooks. The 6 functionalities are used throughout Europe in all kinds of different ways. Curious how you can use Cyric? Let's figure it out together!

WHY SHOULD I USE CYRIC?

Apart from the fact that research has shown that Cyric is much faster than the manual process, you also have the advantage that everything is registered. This provides clarity for yourself, your colleagues and your manager.

The Cyric tool is 100% GDPR proof. We also do our utmost to keep developing and we also take your wishes into account. The question I'm more wondering is, why shouldn’t I use Cyric.
