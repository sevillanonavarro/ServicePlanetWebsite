WHAT IS CYRIC?

Cyric is a toolset with 6 functionalities:

  • Cyric is an advanced platform for automating and managing computers within organizations
  • It supports tasks such as installing, wiping, and testing devices, regardless of brand or model.
  • Cyric operates largely automatically, enabling even non-IT staff to use it with ease
  • A central portal provides real-time insight into the status and history of all devices.
  • The system is fully compliant with privacy and GDPR regulations
  • Cyric saves time and costs, making large-scale computer management reliable and future-proof.

Cyric offers organizations an efficient, secure, and user-friendly way to manage large numbers of computers. With automation and clear reporting, you always have control over your IT environment. This makes professional computer management accessible to everyone.

WHY SHOULD I USE CYRIC?

Cyric stands out as an all-in-one solution for organizations that need to process, prepare, or refurbish large numbers of computers quickly and with consistent quality. The platform is designed for high efficiency: once you start a task, Cyric handles the rest-unattended-so your staff can focus on other work, while minimizing the need for technical expertise. With automated workflows, GDPR-compliant reporting, and a professional, uniform process at every location, Cyric helps you save time, reduce costs, and deliver a better experience to your customers-making it the smart choice for any organization managing IT devices at scale
